# Dogs

I've always had a fondness for dogs. In particular, I really like corgis! Their short legs, fluffy butts, and playful personalities make them incredibly cute and endearing. I especially love how enthusiastic and big-hearted corgis are, even though they might be considered small in size. They seem to have an endless supply of energy and always look like they're smiling.

## What dogs like eating

Most dogs love eating meat, such as chicken or beef, and many of them also enjoy some fruits (like apples or bananas) and vegetables (like carrots). Of course, there are dog foods specifically made for their nutritional needs, but it's important to remember that not all "human" foods are safe for dogs. For example, chocolate, grapes, onions, and certain artificial sweeteners are toxic to them.

## Dog personalities

Some dogs are independent and calm, while others are highly social and thrive on attention from people and other animals.
